I grew up a mile from Vivint's original HQ in Provo, Utah. My final year of high school I landed an internship at the Vivint Innovation Center — in the Space Monkey division, now known as the Vivint Smart Drive.

After my internship, I knocked doors in Oklahoma City in 2017. That's when I realized installing was really my thing. Since then, I've visited many thousands of homes across the country, helping families and neighborhoods feel safe in the places they love most.

My first time in Austin was a 10-day trip that just so happened to coincide with the great freeze of 2021 — Snowmageddon, some may call it. Somehow, after one more work trip to the area, I decided to call it home. I moved here spring of 2023, and I love the opportunity to help this community achieve greater peace of mind through smart, convenient security.

How to reboot my system
  1. Unplug the panel from the wall
  2. If it has a battery backup, hold the power button for 10 seconds
  3. Wait 30 seconds
  4. Plug back in and wait 2 minutes for it to reconnect
  5. For cameras and sensors — remove and reinsert the battery, or power cycle at the breaker
