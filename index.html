Austin Local

I grew up a mile from Vivint's original HQ in Provo, Utah. My final year of high school, I landed an internship at the Vivint Innovation Center — where all the magic is created — in the Space Monkey division, now known as the Vivint Smart Drive.

After my internship, I went and knocked doors in Oklahoma City in 2017. That's when I realized installing was really my thing. Since then, I've visited many thousands of homes across the country, helping families and neighborhoods feel safe in the places they love most.

My first time in Austin was a short 10-day trip that just so happened to be the same time as the great freeze of 2021 — Snowmageddon, some may call it. Somehow, after one more work trip to the area, I decided to call it home. I moved here spring of 2023, and I love the Austin community and the opportunity to help it achieve greater peace of mind through smart, convenient security.
