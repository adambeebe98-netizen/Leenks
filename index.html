Tips & Tricks

Tips to get the most out of your Vivint system. Tap any question to expand.

📱 The App

Open the Vivint app → tap the shield icon. You'll see Disarmed, Away, and Stay. Away arms everything including motion. Stay arms perimeter only — perfect for nighttime when you're home.

Settings → Users → Add User. Set permission levels — Full Control, Arm/Disarm only, or Guest. Give each person their own unique PIN so you can track who armed and when.

Tap the camera → Clips. Filter by camera or date. Tap any clip → download icon to save to your phone. Clips are stored for 30 days — download anything important before then.

📹 Cameras

Almost always insects flying past the IR glow. Fix: lower motion sensitivity slightly, draw a custom activity zone that excludes the area closest to the lens, or move the camera away from outdoor lights.

Tap a camera → gear icon → Activity Zones. Draw a box around the area you care about — driveway, front door, etc. Camera only triggers clips within that zone. This is the biggest thing you can do to reduce false alerts.

WiFi signal issue. The doorbell needs strong 2.4GHz signal.

  • Check signal strength under camera settings
  • Below 60%? Consider a WiFi extender near the front door
  • Make sure internet upload is at least 10Mbps

🚪 Sensors

Put the system in Test Mode first: app → Settings → Test Mode. Walk through every door and window — the panel beeps to confirm each sensor. Auto-disables after 30 minutes.

Common causes: pets (enable Pet Immune mode), HVAC air flow blowing across sensor, afternoon sunlight shifting across field of view, or curtains moving near a window. Try repositioning slightly.

🔒 Smart Home

Yes — Automations → Add Rule → Smart Lock. Set it to lock at a specific time, or trigger when system arms Away. Recommended: when system arms Away → lock all doors. You'll never leave the house unlocked.

Set a schedule: cooler when away, comfortable when home. Link it to the security system — when you arm Away, it adjusts automatically. In Austin summers, 78-80°F away and 72-74°F home adds up to real savings.
